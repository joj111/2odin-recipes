Description

Yogurt is super yummy, you eat it in morning as a breakfast.

Yogurt gives the competitive edge over, let's say oeatmeal, doesn't taste nearly as good as Yogurt!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • *ironically* Yogurt

Steps to make Yogurt

  1. heat milk to 180 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Cool milk to 112-115 degrees fahrenheit
  3. Add your yogurt starter - the good bacteria
  4. Stir the yogurt starter with the rest of the milk
  5. Pour the milk into jars and incubate for 7-9 hours
  6. Place the jars in the fridge to cool and set
